Who we are

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a Christ-centered refuge where people are restored, equipped, and released to impact the nations.

Mission

We make disciples who know God, find freedom, discover purpose, and make a difference through worship, community, and mission.

Values

  • Lordship of Christ
  • Authentic Community
  • Biblical Teaching
  • Excellence in Stewardship
  • Compassion for the Vulnerable
